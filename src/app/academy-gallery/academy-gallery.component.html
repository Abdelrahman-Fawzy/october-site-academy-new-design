Practical training for our academy students is one of the most crucial stages we emphasize in educating and preparing graduates capable of meeting the demands of the job market.

Practical Training is the practical application of what has been taught to the students of the academy through field experiences that equip the students with knowledge and skills about the real work environment they will encounter after graduation.

Throughout the past years, the Academy's management has diligently worked to provide all the necessary resources and means to establish a solid and comprehensive training framework. This framework is characterized by profound concepts, detailed objectives, defined stages, and steps, complementing and fulfilling the various academic curricula.

In pursuit of this objective, the Academy has promptly established partnerships with leading companies, factories, hotels, and organizations, whether in the public or private sector, all relevant entities involved in practical training. This enables the provision of practical training opportunities for the Academy's students.
