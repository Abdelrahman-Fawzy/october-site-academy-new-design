Department of Journalism and Media The Department of Journalism and Media is characterized by teaching subjects related to journalism, radio, television and public relations by a distinguished elite of faculty members and the supporting staff, during two academic years that qualifies the student for the labor market in the field of journalism and media according to the academic and training qualifications required, so that the student becomes able to work As a reporter or editor in paper and electronic newspapers, or as a reporter and broadcaster in both radio and television, in addition to public relations in major institutions and companies.

The department aims to provide students with theoretical and practical information in the field of journalism as follows: Training students on the art and skills of journalistic writing Training students on the art and skills of conducting journalistic investigations Training students on the art and skills of all information, classifying and writing it in news, editing and publishing it Training students on the art and skills of reporting Truth to the public, exposing falsehood and media misinformation - Training students on the art and skills of crafting accurate, objective, clear and ethical media messages - Training students on the art and skills of preparing, writing and publishing scientific research Training students on knowing the regulations, instructions, laws and charters that regulate the work of journalism.
