The Department of Surveying and Maps aims at the following: Graduating qualified technical cadres to carry out ground and photographic survey work, detailed topographical lifting work, establishing networks, calculating excavation and filling quantities, raising land boundaries and calculating its area, as well as leveling works for natural and artificial features of the Earth’s surface using traditional and modern surveying devices (devices The complete station) and the global navigational and cadastral signature devices (GPS), in addition to the preparation and drawing of topographic, real estate, thematic, and detailed maps, as well as the use of geographic information systems (GIS) in order to build a database and produce digital maps in various fields. In addition to traditional scanning devices such as todolites and scales, the department has a large collection of the latest findings of electronic scanning devices, comprehensive station devices, and satellite positioning devices. Ministry of Agriculture, Irrigation and Drainage - Ministry of Health and Population - Ministry of Industry and Trade and the General Authority for Survey, in addition to travel opportunities for all Arab and private countries The department uses the latest computers to produce maps of various shapes and sizes, and the areas, quantities, and coordinates are calculated through programs.
