The aim of the Medical Services Department is to prepare scientific and professional nursing cadres capable of creativity, production of knowledge and competition, preparing distinguished teaching staff scientifically, professionally and culturally, and providing curative and preventive primary health care in the hospitals in which the graduate works - directing first aid cases.
