تسعى أكاديمية 6 أكتوبر فى قسم تكنولوجيا الخدمات الصحية إلى توفير مقومات التطوير المستمر للتعليم فى مجال الخدمات الصحية لمواكبة التطورات العلمية والمهنية المتسارعة فى المجال ، والارتقاء بخريجي القسم إلى مستوى التميز والقدرة التنافسية العالية لتلبية متطلبات سوق العمل من مساعدى الخدمات الصحية فى مصر والمنطقة العربية.

ويُقْصَدُ به القسم المنوظ بالعملية التعليمية الكاملة لطلاب التخصص والمسؤول عن إعداد وتخريج اختصاصى مهنى فى الخدمات الصحية حسب المتطلبات المهنية الواردة في اللائحة.

October 6 Academy seeks in the Department of Health Services Technology to provide the necessary elements for continuous education in the field of healthcare services, keeping pace with the rapid scientific and professional developments in the field, and elevating the department's graduates to a level of excellence and high competitive ability to meet the requirements of the job market for healthcare assistants in Egypt and the Arab region.

The department is intended to carry out the complete educational process for students specializing in this field and is responsible for preparing and graduating professional specialists in healthcare services according to the requirements specified in the regulations.

The department's mission is to technically qualify trainees in the field of health services, aiming to adhere to international standards and levels in the field, keeping up with professional advancements and developments, and focusing on the requirements of the technical market. We strive to provide the best skills related to health services, as demanded by local and international medical and healthcare institutions.
