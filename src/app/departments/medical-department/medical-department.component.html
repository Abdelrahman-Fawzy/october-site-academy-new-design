{{ "DEPART_GOALS" | translate }}

الهدف من قسم الخدمات الطبية هو إعداد كوادر تمريضية علمية و مهنية قادرة على الإبداع و إنتاج المعرفة و المنافسة وإعداد تدريسي متميز علمياً و مهنياً و ثقافياً وتقديم الرعاية الصحية الأولية العلاجية والوقائية بالمستشفيات التى يتم عمل الخريج بها - مباشرة الحالات الإسعافية الأولية. توفر الأكاديمية جميع الاجهزة المساعدة في العملية التعليمية الخاصة بمجال التمريض سواء التقليدية او الحديثة وتوفر فرص التدريب في كبرى المستشفيات.

The aim of the Medical Services Department is to prepare scientific and professional nursing cadres capable of creativity, production of knowledge and competition, preparing distinguished teaching staff scientifically, professionally and culturally, and providing curative and preventive primary health care in the hospitals in which the graduate works - directing first aid cases. The Academy provides all assistive devices in the educational process in the field of nursing, whether traditional or modern, and provides training opportunities in major hospitals.

The department includes a group of qualified and distinguished faculty members with high experience in their work. The Academy is keen to constantly raise the efficiency of the staff in the department, whether by attending seminars, participating in courses, practical and academic studies, or attending medical conferences.

The Department of Medical Services aims to qualify the graduate to be able to carry out various nursing work in all disciplines, to integrate within the medical community and hospitals, to be highly qualified in the field of nursing in all aspects of the profession, and to be familiar with professional ethics and good dealing with patients.
